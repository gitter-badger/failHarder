What have you wanted to do but were too afraid to try?

  • question // March 2015

When I was younger, I wanted to become a ballet dancer. When I started taking ballet classes, I was terrible. So I quit. But I still want to become a dancer, I'm just too afraid of failing.

I identify with that story. But you can do it. Don't be afraid of messing up, because it's okay to mess up as long as you don't let it stop you. The only way you really fail is by never getting to success.
