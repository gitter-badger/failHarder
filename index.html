If you're not failing, you're not growing.

We love to fail. Why? Because if we're failing, we've left our comfort zones. We've pushed ourselves to a limit and refused to stop there. If we're failing, we're experimenting, exploring, discovering, and growing. We're trying new things, and that's exciting. Here at Fail Harder, we don't fear failure. We embrace it. We encourage it. We love it.

Join us as we boldy and fearlessly celebrate failure, our own and each others', as a necessity to great success.

Success is stumbling from failure to failure with no loss of enthusiasm.

Winston S. Churchill
